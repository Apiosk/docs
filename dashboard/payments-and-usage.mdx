# Payments And Usage

Once an API is live, the provider portal gives operators visibility into the commercial side of traffic.

## Where to look

- **Analytics**: paid request volume, total calls, and revenue by API
- **Transactions**: individual settlement and payment records
- **Get paid**: wallet, EUR account, bank details, automatic payouts, manual swap, and payout history

## API-level workflow

For an individual product under **Products**, operators typically use:

- setup
- endpoints
- payments
- gateway

These views help you move from publishing to pricing to ongoing monitoring.
